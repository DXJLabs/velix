# Payment Memo

A payment memo explains what a payment is for.

In VEIL, memos are part of the Deal Room so payment context stays attached to the channel instead of being scattered across external chat tools.

## User Flow

1. Open the payment view from a channel.
2. Review the current deal and escrow status.
3. Choose Shield or Unshield mode.
4. Enter the payment memo.
5. Review the payment context.
6. Send or sign the payment action.

## User Expectations

- A memo should be created before sensitive payment actions when the setting requires it.
- The memo should describe the purpose of the payment clearly.
- The memo should be visible in channel history for the relevant participants.
