# Use Cases

## Private Service Payments

A buyer and seller negotiate scope, attach a payment memo, and coordinate escrow release without exposing commercial details publicly.

## OTC Or Partner Settlement

Counterparties coordinate payment context and proof references while reducing public metadata leakage.

## Grant Or Milestone Work

Teams attach memos and proof references to milestone payments while keeping negotiation context in a private channel.

## Marketplace Escrow

Buyers and sellers use a Deal Room to agree on terms, confirm deposit milestones, and settle when the item or service is delivered.

## Ecosystem Integrations

Wallets, payment tools, marketplaces, and DAO operations products can integrate with VEIL as a private deal coordination layer.
