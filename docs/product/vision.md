# Vision

VEIL makes private deal coordination feel native to Starknet.

The long-term vision is a Deal Room for crypto-native work: users can communicate, negotiate, attach payment context, coordinate escrow, and preserve privacy without leaving the product flow.

## Product Direction

VEIL should become the default private workspace for:

- payment conversations,
- payment memos,
- buyer/seller negotiation,
- escrow coordination,
- proof references,
- privacy-preserving settlement context.

The chain should provide settlement and availability. VEIL should provide the private product layer around the deal.
