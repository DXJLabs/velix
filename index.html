What to explain in interview

VEIL does not rebuild Privacy Pool. It calls the existing pool through the InvokeExternal phase.

The helper stores encrypted timeline payloads only. Sender, recipient, and plaintext stay outside this helper contract.

The research adapter is a bridge. It decodes real transactions now, then gets replaced by official SDK calls later.
